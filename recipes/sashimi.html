How to make sashimi

Description

Homemade Sushi is so much cheaper than at the restaurant. Plus, it's easy and fun to make your own rolls at home,

so you can put all your favorite ingredients into your perfect custom roll — here's how!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ⅓ cups water
  • ⅔ cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 4 sheets nori seaweed sheets
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • ½ cucumber, peeled, cut into small strips

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring water to a boil in a medium pot; stir in rice. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and water has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt in a small bowl. Gently stir into cooked rice in the pot and set aside.

  4. Step 4

    5. Lay nori sheets on a baking sheet.

  5. Step 5

    6. Heat nori in the preheated oven until warm, 1 to 2 minutes.

  6. Step 6

    7. Center 1 nori sheet on a bamboo sushi mat. Use wet hands to spread a thin layer of rice on top. Arrange 1/4 of the crabmeat, avocado, cucumber, and pickled ginger over rice in a line down the center. Lift one end of the mat and roll it tightly over filling to make a complete roll. Repeat with remaining ingredients.

  7. Step 7

    8. Use a wet, sharp knife to cut each roll into 4 to 6 slices.
